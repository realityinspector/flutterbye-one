Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 1, 2025

FLUTTERBYE: The CRM Focused on Healthy Meetings ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when using our service:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, company name, job title, and profile information.
  • Account Information: Username, password, and account preferences.
  • Contact Information: Information about your contacts and leads stored in the CRM.
  • Usage Data: Interactions with the app, features used, and time spent.
  • Health and Activity Data: With your explicit consent, we may collect data related to your physical activities, such as walking meetings, steps taken, or workout plans.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes, including:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our services
  • Processing and completing transactions
  • Sending you technical notices, updates, and support messages
  • Responding to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Providing personalized content and recommendations related to healthy meeting practices
  • Analyzing usage patterns to enhance user experience

3. Health and Activity Data

For features that involve physical activities:

  • Health and activity data is collected on an opt-in basis only
  • You can choose which fitness data to share with the app
  • This data is not shared with third parties without your explicit consent
  • You may delete your health and activity data at any time

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us deliver our services
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property
  • With your consent or at your direction

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the security of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Alpha Status and Data Protection

As FLUTTERBYE is currently in alpha development, we are continuously improving our security measures. We recommend not storing highly sensitive information in the system at this stage of development.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information, including:

  • Accessing, correcting, or deleting your information
  • Objecting to certain processing of your information
  • Requesting portability of your information
  • Withdrawing consent where applicable

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@flutterbye.com.
